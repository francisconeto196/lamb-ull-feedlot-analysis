### Objective  
To evaluate how the inclusion of ULL in lamb diets affects dry matter intake (DMI), average daily gain (ADG), feed efficiency (G:F), and feed costs.

### Methodology  
- **Design:** randomized complete block, 4 treatments (Control, 1.3%, 2.6%, 3.9% ULL).  
- **Software:** R (tidyverse, broom, ggplot2, patchwork).  
- **Outputs:** boxplots, ANOVA summaries, and economic indices.  
- **Scripts:** available in `scripts/analysis.R`.

### Key Findings  
- ULL inclusion slightly increased crude protein intake.  
- Moderate inclusion levels (~2.6% DM) improved nitrogen balance without impairing growth.  
- Economic analysis showed cost/benefit sensitivity to ingredient prices.
